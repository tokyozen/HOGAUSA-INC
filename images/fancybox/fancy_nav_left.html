About Harfordian Sisters

Harfordian Sisters is a cherished association, proudly formed by the esteemed alumnae of Harford School for Girls. Our organization serves as a bridge between past and present, fostering enduring connections among generations of remarkable women who share the common bond of their alma mater. Established with the aim of preserving the rich legacy of Harford School for Girls, we are dedicated to nurturing lifelong friendships, promoting personal and professional growth, and giving back to our beloved institution and community.
