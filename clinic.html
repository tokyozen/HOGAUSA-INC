Our Clinic Project

Background:
Sierra Leone, a country known for its resilience and vibrant culture, faces significant challenges in healthcare infrastructure and resources. Access to quality medical care is often limited, particularly in rural areas where poverty and lack of resources exacerbate existing health disparities. Recognizing these challenges, we embarked on a mission to make a difference by establishing a clinic that would provide essential healthcare services to those in need.

Our Mission:
The goal of the Clinic Project is simple yet profound: to empower communities in Sierra Leone by providing accessible, compassionate, and comprehensive healthcare services. Through this project, we aim to improve health outcomes, enhance quality of life, and foster a culture of wellness and resilience

About HOGAUSA INC

HOGAUSA INC is a cherished association, proudly formed by the esteemed alumnae of Harford School for Girls. Our organization serves as a bridge between past and present, fostering enduring connections among generations of remarkable women who share the common bond of their alma mater. Established with the aim of preserving the rich legacy of Harford School for Girls, we are dedicated to nurturing lifelong friendships, promoting personal and professional growth, and giving back to our beloved institution and community.
